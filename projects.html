Machakos Farm Solar Irrigation Project

Transforming agricultural productivity with sustainable water solutions

The Challenge

A 50-acre farm in Machakos County struggled with severe water shortages during dry seasons, relying on expensive trucking services that increased costs and limited production.

Our Solution

We delivered a full-scale solar irrigation solution including:

  • 120m deep borehole with PVC casing
  • 5HP solar submersible pumping system
  • 50,000L storage tank with elevated stand
  • Drip irrigation across 50 acres
  • Automated control system

Project Results

8,000L/hr Water Yield
60% Cost Savings
Year-round Production
6 months ROI Period

Karen Estate Water Supply Project

Reliable water security for a modern residential community

The Challenge

A luxury 15-unit estate in Karen faced unreliable municipal water supply, forcing residents to rely on costly water bowser services. A permanent, efficient solution was needed.

Our Solution

We implemented a full residential water supply system including:

  • Hydrogeological survey and optimal siting
  • 150m deep borehole drilling
  • Solar-powered submersible pump system
  • 100,000L storage with dual elevated tanks
  • Pressure boosting distribution system
  • Water treatment and filtration plant
